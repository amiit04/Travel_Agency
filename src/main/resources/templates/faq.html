Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking with Ananya Travels? +
To book a tour package with Ananya Travels, browse through our available packages, select your preferred package, and click "Book Now." You can complete the booking by providing necessary details and making the payment on the payment page.
Can I cancel or modify my booking? +
Yes, bookings can be modified or canceled within a specified period prior to the start date. Please refer to our cancellation policy on the booking page or contact our customer support for assistance with modifications.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and online banking. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your safety.
Are there any discounts available? +
Yes, we occasionally offer promotional discounts and special offers. You can apply any valid discount code during booking to get a discount on the total price.
Do you offer travel insurance? +
Yes, we offer optional travel insurance for a nominal fee, providing coverage for trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and more. Contact our support team for more details.
